Quick Start Guide

Get your OLO Dash up and running in minutes

  1. Get OLO Bit Plus
    Use the purchase link at the top of the page to get your OLO Bit Plus hardware. It comes pre-assembled and ready to flash.
  2. Flash Firmware
    Connect the OLO Bit Plus via USB-C, go to the Firmware tab, select your OLED type, and click Install.
  3. Configure WiFi
    Go to the WiFi Setup tab, click "Connect via USB Serial", enter your network credentials, and click "Send to Device". The device will reboot and connect.
  4. Access olodash.local
    On your phone or PC (same WiFi), open http://olodash.local for the full web dashboard with all settings — stocks, crypto, currency, weather, and more.
    🌐 Open Web Dashboard
  5. Enjoy!
    Single-tap to navigate screens, double-tap to open settings, triple-tap for the settings menu, long-press to return home.

Troubleshooting

Device not flashing?
Hold the BOOT button while plugging in USB. Use Chrome or Edge (Web Serial requires Chromium).
Display blank after flash?
Check OLED type selection (SSD1306 vs SH110X). Re-flash with the correct type.
Can't find olodash.local?
Ensure your device is on the same WiFi. Some routers block mDNS — use the IP address shown on the OLED directly.
WiFi credentials not working?
Reconnect via USB Serial (WiFi Setup tab) and re-enter credentials. SSID and password are case-sensitive.
